CINEARC AI Publisher Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 23, 2026

CINEARC AI Publisher is a personal desktop publishing tool used only by the owner of the CINEARC AI Pinterest account.

1. Information Processed

The application may process Pinterest account identifiers, board identifiers, Pin information, titles, descriptions, destination links, media files selected by the user, and Pinterest OAuth access tokens.

2. Purpose of Processing

This information is used only to authenticate the owner's Pinterest account, retrieve boards, upload creator-owned media, and create or manage Pins requested by the owner.

3. Local Storage and Security

Authentication credentials and application settings may be stored locally on the owner's Windows computer. The application is not offered to third parties and does not operate a public user database.

4. Data Sharing

Information is transmitted to Pinterest only when necessary to perform actions requested by the account owner through the Pinterest API. Personal information is not sold to advertisers or unrelated third parties.

5. Media and Content

The application processes only media and metadata selected by the owner. Uploaded content is sent to Pinterest for the purpose of creating Pins on boards controlled by the authenticated account.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Locally stored credentials and settings can be removed from the application or from the operating system. Pinterest access may also be revoked through the owner's Pinterest account settings.

7. Third-Party Services

CINEARC AI Publisher uses the Pinterest API. Information processed by Pinterest is subject to Pinterest's own terms and privacy practices.

8. Children

The application is not directed to children and does not intentionally collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated when the application's functionality or data processing practices change. The effective date will be updated when material changes are made.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or requests concerning locally stored data, contact: anispystory@gmail.com
