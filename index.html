CINEARC Publisher

CINEARC Publisher is a privately operated Windows desktop application used by the owner of the CINEARC creator accounts to prepare and publish creator-owned content.

Privacy Policy CINEARC on YouTube

Pinterest integration

The application reads boards owned by the authenticated Pinterest account and creates standard image Pins using artwork, titles, descriptions and destination links supplied by the account owner.

CINEARC Publisher is a personal creator tool. It is not offered as a public SaaS service and does not access or manage third-party Pinterest accounts.
