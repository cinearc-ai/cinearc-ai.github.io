CINEARC Publisher

CINEARC Publisher is a privately operated Windows desktop application used by the owner of the CINEARC creator accounts to prepare and publish creator-owned content to supported online platforms.

Terms of Service Privacy Policy CINEARC on YouTube

About the application

CINEARC Publisher helps the account owner prepare creator-owned videos, artwork, titles, descriptions, hashtags and destination links for supported publishing platforms.

The application is a personal creator tool. It is not offered as a public SaaS service and does not provide account-management or publishing services to unrelated third parties.

TikTok integration

The application may connect to TikTok through official TikTok APIs to identify the authorized account and publish videos selected by the account owner.

Pinterest integration

The application may read boards owned by the authenticated Pinterest account and create standard image Pins from creator-owned content.

Creator-owned content

Content processed through the application must be owned by the account holder or used with all required permissions.

Local credential storage

Connected-account credentials are stored locally on the owner's Windows computer using operating-system credential storage.
